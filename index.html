FAQ

Questions We
Get Asked Most

Everything you need to know before we start working together. Can't find your answer? Ask us directly →

What does the payment structure look like?
We work on a straightforward 50/50 model — 50% deposit to begin, 50% upon final delivery. For Enterprise engagements, we offer milestone-based payment structures. No hidden recurring fees unless you opt into a support contract.
How long does a typical project take?
Most projects run 6–14 weeks depending on scope. Starter projects can deliver in as few as 4 weeks. We agree on a timeline in writing during the discovery phase, and we stick to it.
Do you work with startups as well as enterprises?
Absolutely. Our portfolio spans pre-seed startups through Fortune 500s. We scale our team and approach to fit your size — you get the same quality of thinking regardless of budget.
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Yes. We handle complex API integrations, legacy system migrations, and custom connector builds. If it has an API or accessible data layer, we can connect it.
What happens after the project launches?
All packages include post-launch support (3–12 months depending on tier). We provide team training, documentation, and ongoing monitoring. You can extend support on a monthly retainer at any time.
Is the free consultation really free?
Yes, completely. The initial discovery call (up to 60 minutes) is free with zero obligation. We'll review your challenge, share our thinking, and give you a clear sense of approach — whether you work with us or not.
